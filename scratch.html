San Diego Padres


Fun Facts

  1. The Padres were founded in 1969 originally as an expansion team
  2. They have made two appearances in the world series (1984 and 1998) however lost both of them and have never won a world series title
  3. Their mascot is the Swinging Friar, and their main colors are brown and gold
  4. The Padres play at Petco Park in downtown San Diego
  5. The San Diego Padres have retired 6 numbers
    • #6 - Steve Garvey (1B)
    • #19 - Tony Gwynn (RF)
    • #31 - Dave Winfield (RF)
    • #35 - Randy Jones (P)
    • #51 - Trevor Hoffman (RP)
    • #42 - Jackie Robinson (2B)
  6. The Padres largest rival is against the Los Angeles Dodgers
